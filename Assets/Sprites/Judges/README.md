## Character Personalities

### The Critic
- Technical perfectionist
- Evaluates: proper technique, ingredient compatibility, edibility
- Penalizes absurd combinations
- Scores conservatively
- Visual: Professional chef attire, glasses, serious expression

### The Visionary
- Avant-garde artist
- Values: creativity, poetic descriptions, unexpected pairings
- Rewards boldness and artistry
- Visual: Eccentric appearance, artistic flair, expressive

### The Soul Cook
- Comfort food champion
- Values: warmth, appetite appeal, heartiness, nostalgia
- Loves dishes that "hug the soul"
- Visual: Warm, grandmotherly, comforting smile, homey apron
