## Art Style Recommendations

Choose a consistent style across all assets:
- **Cartoon/Stylized:** Bold outlines, vibrant colors, fun and approachable
- **Flat/Minimalist:** Clean icons, simple shapes, modern look
- **Realistic:** Photo-based or photorealistic renders
- **Pixel Art:** Retro gaming aesthetic

For a cooking game, warm colors (oranges, reds, yellows) typically work well with kitchen themes.
